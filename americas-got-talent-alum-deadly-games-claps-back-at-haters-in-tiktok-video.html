Deadly Games is a danger act known and loved from America’s Got Talent and America’s Got Talent: The Champions. They are currently performing apart of America’s Got Talent: Live Las Vegas and recently clapped back at haters who suggested their act is rigged.

Commenter Doubts Authenticity of Knife Throwing on Deadly Games’ Viral TikTok

On Dec. 5, Poland’s Got Talent Winner Aleksandra Kiedrowicz shared a video of she and fiancée Alfredo Silva on her TikTok account. The video showed the duo performing a knife throwing act with the song “Face Off” by Dwayne Johnson in the background.

In the video, the knives were getting too close for comfort for a lot of the viewers. This video went beyond viral on TikTok and currently has 2.3 million views. Since it has been broadcasted to a larger audience, it’s not a shock someone is questioning their skills.

Since their act is so impressive, someone wanted to see another angle of their act. In their clapback video, the couple presented a second angle that showed nothing behind the board. Instead, fans got some pretty rad footage of the knives breaking into the wood.

This video hasn’t blown up like the original viral video has, but it proved their point either way (pun intended). Deadly Games does not fake their skills. Fans of the duo are eating up just how cool the second angle made their act.

How This Duo Came to Be

Alfredo has always been apart of Deadly Games, but has continued to perform after separating from his AGT partner and ex-wife, Anna Silva. Before Anna, Alfredo studied magic, juggling, Globe of Death, and knife throwing. At this point in his career, he’s considered to be a seasoned performer. His new partner, Aleksandra Kiedrowicz, was previously an aerial hoop performer on Poland’s Got Talent. They’ve performed as Deadly Games at World’s Got Talent and Britain’s Got Talent: The Champions in 2019.

Now, they’re performing as apart of AGT: Live Las Vegas with other well known AGT alum. Even though Aleksandra hasn’t been Alfredo’s professional partner as long as Anna had been, Deadly Games is still an act of perfection. There’s no way to do knife throwing wrong and still end up alive, so I’m sure the couple caught on to the art very quickly. The adrenaline rush that people feel from their act is because they’ve actually perfected the art.
