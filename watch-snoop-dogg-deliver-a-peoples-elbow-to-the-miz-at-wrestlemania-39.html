WATCH Snoop Dogg Deliver a People's Elbow to The Miz at 'WrestleMania 39'

WrestleMania 39 saw some unexpected turns of events, including the injury of Shane McMahon, who tore his quad during a match with The Miz. However, the show must go on, and rap artist Snoop Dogg stepped up to take McMahon’s place in the ring.

Snoop Dogg Fights The Miz at Wrestlemania 39

Snoop Dogg, who is famous for his music career, has a wrestling background in his family, as he is a cousin of WWE legend Sasha Banks. With his quick thinking, Snoop Dogg managed to save the day and deliver a memorable moment at the event.

In a short video clip from the event we see Snoop Dogg punching The Miz down the ring before performing The Rock’s signature move, The People’s Elbow, on him. The move secured Snoop Dogg his first official WWE victory.

While McMahon’s injury may have been reminiscent of his dad’s injury in 2005, Snoop Dogg’s performance at WrestleMania 39 was a testament to his versatility and ability to adapt to different situations. Despite being known primarily as a musician, Snoop Dogg showed that he has a passion for wrestling and can hold his own in the ring.

Snoop Dogg’s appearance at WrestleMania 39 was one of the highlights of the event. His quick thinking, wrestling background, and passion for the sport made for an entertaining and memorable moment that will go down in WWE history.

Snoop Dogg’s Wrestlemania Involvement Generated Record Sales

Snoop Dogg’s involvement in WrestleMania is just the latest example of celebrities stepping into the ring and not looking out of place. Social media star Logan Paul participated in a WrestleMania match for the second straight year, while San Francisco 49ers tight end George Kittle and rapper Bad Bunny also made appearances.

Despite the unexpected turn of events in Snoop Dogg’s WrestleMania moment, the event still broke records, generating a record gate of $21.6 million and setting single-day attendance records on both nights. While Reigns remained the undisputed champion, the unexpected injury and Snoop Dogg’s surprise appearance added to the excitement of the weekend.

Snoop Dogg‘s WrestleMania moment may not have been planned, but it certainly showcased his talent as an entertainer and his ability to think on his feet. It also proves that anything can happen at WrestleMania, making it one of the most anticipated events in sports entertainment.
